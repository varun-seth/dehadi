Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in our app.

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information about you when you visit our website.

Disclosure

Our App is fully offline and primarily stores all data within your browser's local storage. We do not transmit or share your data to our server or any third-party services. All your data remains on your device unless you choose to back it up or sync it using third-party services like Google Drive or Dropbox.

Dropbox

The application allows you to login with your Dropbox account to enable cloud backup and sync of your data into the storage of your personal Dropbox account.

Consent

This app works by letting you access your Dropbox files via this app. This app will continously read and write to files inside a folder with prefix "Karma", and does not have access to any other files in your Dropbox. By logging in via Dropbox, you consent to this app accessing your Dropbox files.

https://www.dropbox.com/account/connected_apps

Logout

After signing-in via Dropbox, the authorization to access your Dropbox files is kept only in your browser. Logging out will delete this authorization from your browser. Once logged out, this app does not have any access to your Dropbox or any other data.

No Tracking

We do not track you. The app is hosted on GitHub Pages and is purely a frontend application without any backend processing. No login or tracking mechanisms are implemented on our side. No ads are shown. No analytics tools are used.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Dropbox API for accessing Dropbox files. We do not control the privacy practices of Dropbox. Please refer to Dropbox's privacy policy for more information. The Dropbox calls are only initiated after you explicitly click the "Connect to Dropbox" button. And are disabled when you click on "Disconnect from Dropbox". After disconnecting, the app does not have any access to your Dropbox, and no longer calls the Dropbox API.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time, although it is highly unlikely. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
